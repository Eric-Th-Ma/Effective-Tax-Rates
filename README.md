# Real-U.S.-Taxes
This page helps people to understand when, how, and why the effective income tax rate on them and others changed. 
Users can Input various annual income levels (in 2019 dollars) and see:
 - A graph of the effective tax rate over time for 1 or more of these income levels
  - An overlay displaying who had control over the presidency and congress when changes were made
  - An overlay of a number of economic measures on the graph of tax changes
 - Tabulated effective tax rates by year with
  - The change in effective tax rate by year
  - The president, the house, and the senate leaders in each year
  - A number of major tax laws passed that dramatically changed income tax rates
